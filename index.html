Frequently asked questions

How to buy a product?

To buy a product on our house selling site, start by browsing through the available listings to find a property that suits your needs. Once you find a house you're interested in, click on it to view detailed information, including price, features, and photos. You can then contact the seller directly through the provided contact details or our messaging feature to ask questions and schedule a visit to the property. After visiting, if you're satisfied with the house, you can negotiate the price and finalize the terms with the seller. Once an agreement is reached, proceed with the necessary paperwork and legal processes to complete the transaction and transfer ownership. If you need assistance at any point, our support team is ready to help.

How can i make refund from your website?

To request a refund on our website, start by logging into your account and navigating to the "My Purchases" section. Find the transaction related to the property or service for which you need a refund, and click on the "Request Refund" button. You will be prompted to provide a reason for your refund request, along with any relevant details or supporting documentation. Once submitted, our team will review your request and process it according to our refund policy. You will be notified via email about the status of your refund, and the amount will be credited back to your original payment method if approved. For any assistance during this process, our customer support team is available to help.

I am a new user. How should I start?

As a new user on our house selling site, the first step is to create an account by clicking on the "Sign Up" or "Register" button. Fill in your personal details, including your name, email address, and a secure password. Once your account is set up, explore the site to familiarize yourself with the available features. You can start by browsing through property listings to get an idea of what's on offer. If you find a property that interests you, you can save it to your favorites, contact the seller, or even schedule a viewing. If you're planning to sell a property, you can easily list it by following the prompts in the "Sell" section. Our website is designed to be user-friendly, but if you need any guidance, our support team is just a click away to assist you.

Returns and refunds

Our returns and refunds policy is designed to ensure a smooth experience for our users. If you're unsatisfied with a property or service purchased through our site, you can request a refund by logging into your account and navigating to the "My Purchases" section. From there, select the relevant transaction and click on "Request Refund." You'll need to provide a reason for the refund and any supporting information. Our team will review your request in accordance with our refund policy and respond as soon as possible. If approved, the refund will be processed and credited back to your original payment method. Please note that certain terms and conditions may apply, and the refund process may vary depending on the nature of the transaction. If you have any questions or need assistance, our customer support team is available to help.

Are my details secured?

Yes, your details are secure on our website. We take your privacy and security very seriously, employing industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal information. All sensitive data, such as your login credentials and payment information, are encrypted to prevent unauthorized access. We also regularly update our security measures to stay ahead of potential threats. Additionally, we adhere to strict privacy policies, ensuring that your data is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. You can trust that your information is handled with the utmost care and security.

How do I make payment by my credit card?

To make a payment with your credit card on our website, start by selecting the property or service you wish to purchase and proceed to checkout. On the payment page, choose "Credit Card" as your payment method and enter your card details, including the card number, expiration date, CVV code, and the name on the card. Once you review and confirm the information, submit your payment. After the transaction is processed successfully, you’ll receive a confirmation on the screen and an email receipt detailing your purchase. If you need any assistance during the payment process, our customer support team is here to help.
